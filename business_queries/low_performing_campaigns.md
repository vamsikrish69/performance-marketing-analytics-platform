# Low Performing Campaigns

## Business Question

Which campaigns are not generating satisfactory results?

## Objective

Identify campaigns producing low returns on advertising spend and poor performance metrics.

## SQL File

```text
analyses/low_performing_campaigns.sql
```

## Metrics Used

- Spend
- Revenue
- ROAS
- ROI
- Conversion Rate

## Business Value

This analysis helps:

- Detect inefficient campaigns
- Reduce wasted advertising spend
- Improve marketing efficiency
- Optimize campaign performance

## Expected Outcome

Campaigns with poor returns are highlighted so that marketing teams can pause, optimize, or redesign them.

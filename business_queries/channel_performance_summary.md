# Channel Performance Summary

## Business Question

Which marketing channel delivers the strongest performance?

## Objective

Compare channel-level marketing performance between paid advertising platforms.

## SQL File

```text
analyses/channel_performance_summary.sql
```

## Metrics Used

- Total Spend
- Revenue
- Conversions
- ROAS
- ROI
- CAC
- CTR
- Conversion Rate

## Business Value

This analysis helps:

- Evaluate channel effectiveness
- Compare marketing performance
- Identify scalable acquisition channels
- Improve channel mix optimization

## Expected Outcome

Marketing channels are compared side-by-side to determine which platforms produce the best return on advertising investment.

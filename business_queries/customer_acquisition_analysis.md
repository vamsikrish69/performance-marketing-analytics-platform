# Customer Acquisition Analysis

## Business Question

What customer acquisition trends can be identified from marketing activities?

## Objective

Analyze customer conversion behavior, revenue generation, and customer value.

## SQL File

```text
analyses/customer_acquisition_analysis.sql
```

## Metrics Used

- Customer Count
- Conversion Count
- Revenue
- Customer Value Segments
- Conversion Status

## Business Value

This analysis helps:

- Understand customer behavior
- Identify valuable customer segments
- Improve acquisition strategies
- Increase customer lifetime value

## Expected Outcome

Customers are evaluated based on acquisition, conversion activity, and generated revenue to support customer-focused marketing decisions.

# Top Campaigns by ROAS

## Business Question

Which campaigns generate the highest Return on Advertising Spend (ROAS)?

## Objective

Marketing teams need to identify campaigns producing the highest returns so that additional budget can be allocated to the most profitable campaigns.

## SQL File

```text
analyses/top_campaigns_by_roas.sql
```

## Metrics Used

- Total Spend
- Total Revenue
- ROAS
- ROI
- Conversions

## Business Value

This analysis helps:

- Identify top-performing campaigns
- Prioritize marketing investments
- Increase advertising efficiency
- Improve budget allocation decisions

## Expected Outcome

Campaigns are ranked from highest to lowest ROAS, allowing stakeholders to quickly identify the best-performing marketing initiatives.
